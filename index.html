About Us

Welcome to Rocky Turbines, where a childhood fascination with the wind evolved into a pioneering venture in the heart of Crestwood, Missouri. Founded in 1999 by a group of friends whose shared enthusiasm for wind energy began with Lego models of wind turbines, our journey from playroom creations to real-world innovation has been nothing short of remarkable.
Growing up in Crestwood, our founders developed a deep curiosity for the power of the wind. What started as an innocent hobby crafting wind turbine replicas from Lego bricks transformed into a lifelong passion. Fueled by this passion, we embarked on educational journeys, all majoring in mechanical engineering, with a singular vision in mind â€“ harnessing the wind's potential to shape a sustainable future.
In 1999, Rocky Turbines emerged as the embodiment of our collective dream. Nestled in the serene landscapes of Crestwood, we embarked on a mission to redefine the wind energy landscape. Our commitment to quality, innovation, and environmental responsibility has propelled us to the forefront of the renewable energy sector.
At Rocky Turbines, we believe in the extraordinary impact that a childhood dream can have when nurtured with dedication and expertise. Join us on our journey as we continue to harness the power of the wind, driving positive change for generations to come.
